how it works

Write a tree once.
Run it every day.

You define the questions. You define the routes. The app remembers every run, surfaces your patterns, and holds your initiatives accountable — automatically.

01 /
Write your tree in plain text
Define nodes with a minimal DSL — questions, options, routing logic. Single choice, multi-select, or free text. Connect them however makes sense for your life.
02 /
Run it daily from your pocket
A daily notification brings you back. Tap through the tree in minutes. Your phone is always with you — that's the point. Reflection shouldn't require opening a laptop.
03 /
See patterns emerge over time
Every run is saved. The dashboard shows your streak, your KPI graphs, your consistency heatmap. The tree is the ritual. The dashboard is the accountability.
04 /
Evolve your tree as you grow
Visual node editor, version history, import/export. Your tree grows with you. Nothing is locked. Everything is yours.

features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

▶
Structured test mode
Card-by-card walkthrough. Single choice, multi-select, and free-text nodes. Back navigation. Inline history from past runs.
◈
Visual canvas
Pan, zoom, and tap to select. Your tree rendered as a live graph so you can see how it all connects. Builds as you write.
✦
Node editor
Add, edit, and remove nodes without touching the source. Dropdown destination picker. @set and @if helpers built in.
◎
Initiative dashboard
Contribution heatmap. Current and max streaks. Per-project KPI graphs with weekly delta — increasing or decreasing, explicit.
⏰
Daily notifications
Set a daily reminder. Tap the notification and land directly in test mode. Your phone's proximity is a feature, not a distraction.
⟳
Version history
Autosaved every 2 seconds. 7-day rolling window. Split preview panel. One-tap restore. Accidentally deleted everything? Covered.
philosophy
The tree is the ritual.
The dashboard is the accountability.
Proximity as a superpower
Your phone is always within reach. Wisdom Tree is built specifically for that proximity — a daily reflection ritual that takes two minutes, lives in your pocket, and gets smarter the longer you use it. Desktop apps ask too much. This asks just enough.
Your structure, not ours
We don't tell you what to ask yourself. You write the tree. Single questions, multi-path explorations, variables that track across runs — it's a tool that adapts to the shape of your thinking, not the other way around.
Initiatives, not journaling
The dashboard isn't about the tree — it's about your projects. Track a KPI. See a 30-day contribution graph. Watch the weekly delta trend upward. Accountability comes from seeing the numbers, not from writing about your feelings.
Gets better as time goes on
The longer you run your tree, the more signal you accumulate. Past answers surface inline. Patterns become visible. Your streaks become something worth protecting. It compounds.
